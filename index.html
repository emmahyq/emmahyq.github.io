Emma Huang is a 15 y/o frontend developer and graphics designer based in the Pacific Northwest.

She's currently executive director (and occasional piano instructor) at music tutoring organization Verhythm Studio.

Verhythm Studio

An online music studio dedicated to providing accessible and available music education for youth.

I founded this organization with a few of my friends in January 2021 in response to the shift to online learning and rocketing prices of music lessons. We wanted to provide aspiring musicians a quality place to further their learning regardless of financial status.

Safe Mindspace

Your one-stop shop for mental health and relaxation, completely online.

This was my first venture into attending hackathons - my friends and I were encouraged to make a mental health resource after seeing the toll it took on ourselves and other people, especially during the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Librowser

A free bookmarking tool to help bridge the gap between paper and electronic study resources.

Another hackathon project, this was one of the most complicated projects that I've ever contributed to - it was inspired by the upcoming AP season and the difficulties we had with organizing our study materials.
